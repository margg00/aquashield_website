The problem

Undetected water leaks are a major cause of property damage in commercial and multi-unit residential buildings. They drive billions in repair costs, insurance claims, and operational disruption.

Our solution

AquaShield provides non-invasive, AI-powered water leak detection and localization using existing meters and clamp-on sensors. No pipe cutting. No disruption.

Who it’s for

  • Large real-estate portfolios
  • Facility & maintenance teams
  • Insurers and risk managers

Pilots & validation

Pilots ongoing with academic and institutional partners (MIT, EPFL), with commercial pilots starting in large residential portfolios.
